# ⚠️ **Risk Register & Mitigation Plan**

This document outlines key business, technical, operational, and financial risks for Pollinations.AI, along with corresponding mitigation strategies, phased according to roadmap milestones. It is a living document and should be reviewed periodically.

**Roadmap Phases for Reference:**
*   **Phase 1: Foundation & Early Adoption (Current - Q2 2025):** Explorer Tier (Live), Creator Tier (Beta Hardening)
*   **Phase 2: Early Monetization & Tier Progression (Q3 2025 - Q4 2025):** Innovator Tier (GA), ROI Dashboard, Pollinations Ad SDK v1
*   **Phase 3: Premium Offerings & Enhanced Monetization (Q1 2026 - Q2 2026):** Partner Tier (GA), SOTA Models, Rich Ad Formats
*   **Phase 4: Full Ecosystem & Revenue Sharing (Q3 2026 - Q4 2026):** Associate Tier (GA), 50/50 Ad-Revenue Share, Advanced Ad Mediation
