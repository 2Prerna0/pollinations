# ⚠️ **Risk Register & Mitigation Plan**

This document outlines key business, technical, operational, and financial risks for Pollinations.AI, along with corresponding mitigation strategies, phased according to the strategic roadmap. It is a living document and should be reviewed periodically.

**Roadmap Phases for Reference:**
*   **Phase 1: Foundation & Initial Monetization (H2 2025):** Innovator Tier (GA), ROI Dashboard, Ad SDK v1, SOTA Models & Rich Ads for Innovators.
*   **Phase 2: Building for Partnership & Advanced Ads (H1 2026):** Ad SDK v2, Rev-Share Ledger & Payout System Dev, Partner Tier (Closed Beta), Advanced Ad Mediation Dev.
*   **Phase 3: Full Ecosystem Launch – Revenue Share & Scale (H2 2026):** Partner Tier (GA) with 50/50 Net Revenue Share, Automated Payouts, Public Rev-Share Program Launch.
